Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new battery to track?
Tap the "+" button in the app, select your battery type (AA, AAA, etc.), and enter the details like brand, purchase date, and current location. You can also add a photo to help identify it later.
Can I track rechargeable batteries?
Yes! The app is perfect for tracking rechargeable batteries. You can log charge cycles, set reminders for recharging, and track the battery's performance over time.
Is my data backed up?
If you have iCloud enabled, your battery data is automatically backed up and restored when you are setting up a new phone by transferring, or when you restore the app after a system offload.
